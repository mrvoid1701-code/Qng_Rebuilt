# Semiclassical Interface

Type: `note`
ID: `NOTE-BRIDGE-002`
Status: `draft`
Author: `C.D Gabriel`

## Objective

Define what counts as a legitimate GR-QM interface before the QNG core tries to instantiate one.

## Minimal interface idea

The clean semiclassical interface is:

- geometry remains classical or effective
- matter sector may be quantized
- expectation values or effective stress terms source geometric response

## Questions that belong here

- what is treated classically
- what is treated quantum mechanically
- what back-reaction means
- what perturbative order is being claimed

## Questions that do not belong here yet

- what the fundamental substrate is
- whether spacetime itself is a graph
- whether the metric is emergent from another field

Those belong only after QNG pure begins.
