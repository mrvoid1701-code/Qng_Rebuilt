## Backbone objects

At this layer, a propagator is a Green-type object generated by the Hamiltonian or the field operator.

Minimal examples:

- time-evolution kernel
- two-point function
- vacuum correlator

## Clean separation

This file does not yet decide:

- graph Laplacian representation
- discrete mode basis
- QNG-native substrate

Those decisions belong downstream.

## Why it matters

In the old repo, propagator language and graph language were often introduced together.  
Here they stay separate first, so later graph discretization can be evaluated as a representation choice rather than a primitive fact.
