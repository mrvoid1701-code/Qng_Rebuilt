# GR Observables v1

Type: `definition`
ID: `DEF-GR-002`
Status: `draft`
Author: `C.D Gabriel`

## Objective

Define the GR-facing observables that later layers are allowed to compare against.

## Exact objects

Exact geometric observables include:

- curvature invariants
- geodesic deviation
- causal structure
- conserved quantities from symmetries

## Weak-field observables

Weak-field observables include:

- effective Newtonian potential
- lapse perturbation
- spatial metric perturbation
- radial acceleration signatures

## PPN / proxy observables

Proxy observables include:

- `gamma`
- `beta`
- Shapiro-type contrast
- equivalence-principle proxy quantities

## Rule for later layers

If QNG or phenomenology later claims agreement with GR, it must specify:

- exact-level agreement
- weak-field agreement
- or proxy-level agreement

These are not interchangeable.
