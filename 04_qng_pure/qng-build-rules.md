# QNG Build Rules

Type: `note`
ID: `NOTE-QNG-001`
Status: `draft`
Author: `C.D Gabriel`

## Objective

Fix the build discipline for QNG pure before the theory body grows.

## Rules

1. QNG may not redefine GR primitives.
2. QNG may not redefine QM primitives.
3. QNG may introduce native substrate objects only after the bridge layer exists.
4. QNG may define emergent geometry only after stating what is primitive and what is derived.
5. QNG may not use phenomenological success as proof of ontology.

## Interpretation

This means:

- data support can motivate a theory layer
- but it cannot secretly define the theory layer

## Failure pattern to avoid

The legacy workspace sometimes allowed:

- emergent metric definitions
- test-friendly effective equations
- and phenomenological fit terms

to grow together.

In this rebuild they must be separable.
