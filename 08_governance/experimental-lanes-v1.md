# Experimental Lanes v1

Type: `note`
ID: `NOTE-GOV-007`
Status: `draft`
Author: `C.D Gabriel`

## Objective

List the parts of the rebuilt workspace that should remain explicitly experimental so they do not contaminate the provisional backbone.

## Lane 1

`exact recovery theorems`

Status:

- experimental

Reason:

- both GR and QM recovery programs are moving
- neither side has reached theorem level

## Lane 2

`continuity-style QM balance`

Status:

- experimental

Reason:

- the source-free version is weak
- the source-augmented version is still weak
- this lane should be used for exploration, not backbone claims

## Lane 3

`mass semantics of chi`

Status:

- experimental

Reason:

- `chi = m/c` is still structurally fragile
- no rebuild result currently justifies hardening it

## Lane 4

`tau unification`

Status:

- experimental

Reason:

- native memory
- effective lag
- and phenomenological delay remain only partially linked

## Lane 5

`proxy-to-data mapping`

Status:

- experimental

Reason:

- the rebuild has strong internal proxies
- but real-data contact can easily reintroduce hidden assumptions

## Lane 6

`full matter interpretation`

Status:

- experimental

Reason:

- a matter-like proxy exists
- but matter ontology and source semantics remain open

## Operating rule

Anything in these lanes may:

- motivate new tests
- motivate new derivations
- motivate refactoring of open assumptions

But it may not:

- redefine the provisional backbone
- silently change the freeze candidate
- or be cited as settled theory
